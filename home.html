Microphone Information

A microphone, colloquially nicknamed mic or mike is a transducer that
converts sound into an electrical signal.

A microphone can be used for multiple things! Condenser Microphones
are most commonly used for recording lead vocal tracks, live strings,
pianos, and acoustic guitars. These microphones are usually
more expencive like the Blue Microphones Bottle Mic Locker Package
which included the microphone itself with a blue bottle condenser and
4 seperate microphone which all have different settings.

There are many for easy use which you could record top notch
commentary with like the Blue Snowball USB Microphone (Textured White)
which is very solid and comes with 3 settings, one which picks up little
sound and you have to be very close to microphone, two which picks up
more sound but not all sound in the room, and three which picks up almost
all the sound in he room and isn't useful except for maybe skype. There is another variation called the Blue Snowball iCE Condenser Microphone, Cardioid - White which only has setting two which may be the only one you need for commentary.

The Blue Yeti USB Microphone - Silver which is just a couple steps up from the Blue Snowball and a great competitor with many other microphones but it is still very far down the list from the Blue Bottle.
